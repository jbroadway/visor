Helpers are handlers that can be reused in other apps. Helpers are one of the ways Elefant enables reusability and creates more composable apps.

Helpers can be called via PHP or in templates in the same way that dynamic objects can be included in pages and blocks.

For more information about helpers, visit the server-side or client-side pages.
